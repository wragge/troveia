What's Troveia

Troveia is an online trivia competition. It's an opportunity to have some fun and explore Trove's vast content. Unlike most trivia competitions you'll have to use your search skills rather than just your memory. The answer to each question resides in a Trove record, find it and you'll score a point.

How to join in

Just register a team before kick off time. Then come back here on Friday night at 7.00pm to view the questions.

Keep an eye on our Twitter feed throughout the night for announcements, hints and special spot prizes.
